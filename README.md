# Brazilian E-Commerce Data Analysis (Olist Dataset)

## Data Science Portfolio Project

---

## Project Overview

This project is an end-to-end data analysis of a real-world Brazilian e-commerce dataset from Olist.

The goal is to extract meaningful business insights about customer behavior, revenue patterns, delivery performance, and customer satisfaction.

I follow a full data science workflow:
  Data Cleaning  
  Feature Engineering   
  Exploratory Data Analysis  
  Advanced Analytics   
  Business Insights   

---

## Business Objective

Aim to understand what drives:

    Revenue generation  
    Customer satisfaction  
    Delivery performance  
    Customer behavior  
    Customer retention  

---

## Key Business Questions

  What factors influence customer satisfaction?
  Who are the most valuable customers (VIPs)?
  How does delivery time affect reviews?
  How is revenue distributed across customers?
  Do payment methods affect spending?
  Are there seasonal sales patterns?
  What drives customer retention?

   Source: Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset (Olist)
   Type: Real-world e-commerce transactions

## Key Insights

### Revenue Concentration
A small group of customers generates most of the revenue.

### Low Retention
Most customers purchase only once, showing retention challenges.

### Delivery Impact
Longer delivery times lead to lower customer satisfaction.

### VIP Customers
High-value customers strongly influence total revenue.

### Time Patterns
Sales vary by day and month, showing seasonal trends.

---

## Advanced Insight

Customer engagement is the strongest driver of revenue and retention.

High-value customers are defined more by engagement than demographics.
